Chicken Curry (Bangladeshi Style)

Ingredients

  • Chicken – 1 kg (cut into pieces)
  • Onion – 3 large, thinly sliced
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 4
  • Turmeric powder – 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tbsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Bay leaves, cardamom, cinnamon – for aroma
  • Oil – ½ cup
  • Water – as needed

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and add whole spices (bay leaves, cardamom, cinnamon).
  2. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry for a minute.
  4. Add chicken pieces and fry until browned.
  5. Mix in turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
  6. Cook covered for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Add water for desired gravy consistency and simmer until chicken is tender.
  8. Finish with green chilies and cook for another 5 minutes.
