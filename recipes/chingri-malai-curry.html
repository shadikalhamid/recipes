Chingri Malai Curry (Prawns in Coconut Gravy)

Ingredients

  • Prawns – 500g
  • Coconut milk – 2 cups
  • Onion paste – 2 tbsp
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric – ½ tsp
  • Garam masala – ½ tsp
  • Oil – 3 tbsp
  • Salt – to taste

Instructions

  1. Lightly fry prawns with turmeric and salt.
  2. Fry onion and ginger-garlic paste in oil.
  3. Add coconut milk and spices, stir well.
  4. Add prawns and simmer until the curry thickens.
