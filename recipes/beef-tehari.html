Beef Tehari

Ingredients

  • Beef – 1 kg
  • Basmati rice – 3 cups
  • Onions – 2 (sliced)
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Yogurt – ½ cup
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Black pepper – 1 tsp
  • Bay leaves, cardamom, cinnamon – for aroma
  • Salt and oil – as needed

Instructions

  1. Fry onions until golden.
  2. Add beef, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
  3. Cook with yogurt and a bit of water until beef is tender.
  4. Boil rice with whole spices until 70% done.
  5. Layer rice over the beef, add a bit of oil or ghee.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 15–20 minutes (dum).
