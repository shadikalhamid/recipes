Shorshe Ilish (Hilsa in Mustard Sauce)

Ingredients

  • Hilsa fish slices -- 4 to 5
  • Mustard seeds -- 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies -- 4 to 5
  • Mustard oil -- 4 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder -- Â½ tsp
  • Salt -- to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak mustard seeds and green chilies, then grind into a paste
  2. Marinate fish with salt and turmeric
  3. Heat mustard oil, add mustard paste, and cook a bit
  4. Add water and bring to a boil
  5. Add fish slices and cook until done
  6. Add green chilies and a little raw mustard oil before serving
