Begun Bharta (Mashed Eggplant)

Ingredients

  • Eggplant – 1 large
  • Mustard oil – 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 2 to 3
  • Onion – 1, finely chopped
  • Salt – to taste

Instructions

  1. Roast eggplant over open flame or bake until soft.
  2. Peel the skin and mash well.
  3. Mix with salt, chopped onion, green chilies, and mustard oil.
  4. Serve with plain rice.
